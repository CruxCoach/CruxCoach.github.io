What it does

Built around the things you actually do at the wall — not features the official app needed but you don't.

B

Browse 85,000+ climbs

Filter by grade, angle, quality, move count, setter. Works offline once the board database is synced.

L

Light up holds via Bluetooth

Direct BLE to your Kilter Board. Pick custom colors per role: start, hand, foot, top.

S

Search by hold

Tap holds on the board image. Find every climb that uses that exact combination.

L

Local, encrypted logbook

Sends, attempts, opinions, sessions — all stored on your device in an SQLCipher database. Never leaves unless you ask.

N

Share with climbers nearby

Send the climb you're projecting to a friend over Bluetooth. No internet needed. No account on either side.

↻

Import your old data

Got a Kilter logbook export? CruxCoach reads it directly. Your training history doesn't have to die with the official app.

Why this exists.

On 26 March 2026, Aurora's Kilter app was pulled from the App Store and Play Store after a dispute between Aurora Climbing and Kilter, LLC. Tens of thousands of climbers lost access to their playlists, logbooks, and draft climbs overnight. The new official app shipped half-finished, and people started asking the obvious question: why does my training data live on someone else's server in the first place?

CruxCoach is one answer to that question. Your training history sits encrypted on your phone. Your identity is a Nostr keypair you control, not an account someone can revoke. The board database is content-addressed and distributed peer-to-peer, so even the maintainer of CruxCoach can't shut it down. And the whole thing is open source under the GPL — fork it, audit it, run it forever.

It's narrower than the official app on purpose: Kilter only, Android only. But it's yours.

Privacy by architecture, not by promise

Privacy claims are easy. CruxCoach is built so the privacy holds even if we're lying about it.

  • No accounts, no signup. Your identity is a Nostr keypair generated on first launch, stored in the Android Keystore.
  • No Google Play Services. Works on de-Googled phones — GrapheneOS, /e/OS, LineageOS without GApps.
  • Encrypted local storage. Personal data lives in an SQLCipher database. Encryption key never leaves your device.
  • No telemetry, no analytics, no ads. The app makes no network calls except to the Bluetooth board, and to Nostr relays you choose.
  • No central server. There's nothing for us to shut down — or be made to shut down.
  • Verifiable open-source builds. Code on Codeberg, releases signed, auto-updater pins the developer key on first install.

For developers

CruxCoach is GPLv3, written in Kotlin Multiplatform with a Jetpack Compose Android frontend. Issues and PRs live on Codeberg.
